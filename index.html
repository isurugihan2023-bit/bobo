Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

A comprehensive suite of audio features designed to provide crystal clear music, interactive controls, and endless entertainment.

Advanced Music System

Crystal clear audio, custom playlists, interactive UI panels, queue jumping, and DJ roles to control the party.

Audio Filters & Radio

Apply real-time filters like Bassboost, Nightcore, 8D, and Lofi. Listen to live FM radios seamlessly without losing your current queue.

Autoplay & 24/7 Mode

Never let the music stop! Bot automatically picks related songs or stays in your voice channel 24/7.

User Entrance Themes

Set your personal WWE-style entrance music that plays automatically for 10 seconds when you join a voice channel.

Interactive UI Panels

Control everything via buttons and dropdowns! Skip, pause, apply filters, and jump to specific songs in the queue without typing a single command.

Personal Playlists

Create, save, and load your own personal playlists directly in Discord. Carry your favorite songs across any server Bobo is in!

Commands

Music Commands

Explore the vast library of commands Bobo has to offer.

Music Commands

High fidelity music streaming

!play

Play a song from YouTube, Spotify, or URL.

!join

Connect the bot to your voice channel without playing music.

!skip

Skip the currently playing song.

!queue

View the current music queue.

!247

Toggle 24/7 mode in the voice channel.

!settheme

Set your personal entrance theme song.

!panel

Spawn the interactive UI player control panel.

!playlist

Save the current queue as a permanent playlist.

!load

Load and play your saved personal playlists.
